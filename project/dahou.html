Dahou

UI/UX Design
Client － MBI Lab, NCKU
2017.10

"Dahou" in Chinese means to join a meal as a partner with someone. This is an app designed for people to join other's meal easier.


Dahou

Three tabs overview of Dahou. The left one for everybody to join meals; the middle one for chefs to release the date, place and meals to eat; the right one for users to check the app settings.


Tab: Join Meals

For users to join meals, check vacancies, place and the price. Users can also use filter to screen out the meal type they want.


Tab: Release Meals

For chefs to release information, including meals, vacancies, place and the price. Chefs can also use attracting photos to catch everybody's eyes.


Tab: Settings

Settings tab allows users to turn on/off the notification, and gives them the ability to check the meals joined and joining.
